关于我 / About me

我是徐子涵，一位充满热情且注重细节的艺术与设计实践者，拥有扎实的创意视觉艺术基础。在艺术与设计领域，我通过不断学习和对创作的热爱，将创意与自律相结合，创作出既能体现个人艺术视角又能打动不同观众的作品。除了技术能力之外，我还注重团队合作、适应能力和积极的心态，这让我在团队项目和个人挑战中都能表现出色。

I am Zihan Xu, a passionate and detail-oriented art and design practitioner, with a strong foundation in creative visual arts. My journey in art and design has been shaped by my dedication to continuous learning and my ability to balance creativity with discipline. I take pride in creating works that not only reflect my personal artistic vision but also connect with diverse audiences. Beyond my technical skills, I value teamwork, adaptability, and a positive outlook, which help me excel in collaborative projects and individual challenges alike.
